Features

Built for real events

Every detail designed to keep hosts in control and guests happy.

Multiple QR codes
Create several codes per album — one for the ceremony, one for the reception. Enable or disable each independently.
Face detection
Filter your gallery by detected faces. Find every photo of a specific person in seconds — visible to hosts only.
Time-gated uploads
Albums open and close on the dates you choose. No late submissions, no manual closing.
ZIP bulk download
Export every photo and video in one click. Original quality, full resolution, organized by upload time.
Welcome card
A branded landing page with your event title, message, cover photo, and location — the first thing guests see.
Guest gallery toggle
Choose whether guests can browse all uploads together, or keep everything private until you share it.
